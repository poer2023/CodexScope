# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want CodexScope to be a practical, respectful project for people improving a
developer tool. Contributors and maintainers are expected to participate in a
way that is professional, constructive, and welcoming.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful of differing experience levels and technical opinions.
- Keep discussion focused on the project and its users.
- Give actionable feedback with enough context for someone to respond.
- Respect privacy. Do not post another person's private logs, credentials,
  account data, or personal information.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Repeated off-topic disruption after a maintainer asks for it to stop.
- Deliberately misleading security reports or attempts to coerce maintainers.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments, issues, pull requests, or other
contributions that violate this code of conduct. Maintainers may also limit or
block participation when needed to protect the project and its contributors.

To report a conduct concern, contact the maintainer through the GitHub profile
linked from the repository owner. For security-sensitive material, follow
[SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) instead of posting publicly.
